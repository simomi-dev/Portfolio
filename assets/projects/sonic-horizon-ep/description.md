# Sonic Horizons EP

## About This Project

This EP is a deep exploration into ambient electronic music, blending granular synthesis, modular sound design, and heavily processed field recordings into immersive sonic landscapes. Each track was crafted as a journey — starting from silence, building through layered textures of reverb-drenched pads and glitched-out rhythms, and arriving at a space where the listener can feel the music rather than just hear it.

The production process involved extensive experimentation with FM synthesis, wavetable modulation, and spatial audio techniques. Every sound was sculpted from scratch using a combination of hardware synthesizers and software instruments, then mixed in a way that emphasizes depth, space, and emotional resonance.

## Tools & Technologies

- FL Studio 21
- Serum / Vital (Wavetable Synthesis)
- Granular Processing (Quanta, Portal)
- Analog Lab V
- iZotope Ozone (Mastering)

# Visual Identity Pack

## About This Project

The Visual Identity Pack is a full-spectrum branding deliverable that provides everything a new brand needs to launch with a polished, professional presence. It goes beyond logo design to encompass the complete visual ecosystem: typography pairings, color systems with accessibility-checked contrast ratios, iconography, photography direction, and layout templates for both print and digital applications.

Each element in the pack is designed to work both independently and as part of a cohesive system. The guidelines document serves as the single source of truth for brand consistency, providing clear rules and flexible frameworks that allow creative teams to produce on-brand materials without bottlenecking on design review.

## Tools & Technologies

- Adobe Illustrator (Brand Marks)
- Adobe InDesign (Guidelines Document)
- Figma (Digital Templates)
- Coolors (Color System)
- Google Fonts (Typography Curation)

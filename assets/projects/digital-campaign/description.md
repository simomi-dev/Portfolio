# Digital Campaign

## About This Project

This project encompasses a complete social media branding and content strategy developed for an emerging lifestyle brand. The campaign spans multiple platforms — Instagram, TikTok, and X (Twitter) — with a unified visual language that adapts to each platform's unique format and audience expectations while maintaining a consistent brand voice.

The strategy combines data-driven audience analysis with creative experimentation. Content pillars were established around the brand's core values, and a modular design system was created to enable rapid content production without sacrificing visual quality. From carousel posts and Stories templates to short-form video aesthetics and engagement-optimized captions, every element was designed to drive measurable growth.

## Tools & Technologies

- Figma (Template Design)
- Adobe Photoshop (Photo Editing)
- CapCut / Premiere Pro (Video Content)
- Later (Scheduling & Analytics)
- Notion (Content Calendar)

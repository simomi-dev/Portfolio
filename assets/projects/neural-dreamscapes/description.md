# Neural Dreamscapes

## About This Project

Neural Dreamscapes is an ongoing series of AI-generated visual art that explores the boundaries between human creativity and machine intelligence. Each piece begins with a carefully crafted prompt — a fusion of art historical references, emotional cues, and abstract concepts — and is then refined through iterative generation, inpainting, and post-processing until it achieves a surreal, otherworldly quality.

The collection draws inspiration from Romanticism's sublime landscapes, Surrealist dream logic, and the cosmic visions of science fiction concept art. By pushing generative models to their expressive limits and combining outputs from multiple AI systems, the work creates impossible vistas that feel simultaneously familiar and alien — landscapes that could only exist in the space between human imagination and neural network computation.

## Tools & Technologies

- Midjourney v6
- Stable Diffusion XL (ComfyUI)
- Leonardo AI
- Adobe Firefly
- Photoshop (Post-processing & Compositing)

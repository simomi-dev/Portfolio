Halifax Explosion of 1917

This story-driven video about the Halifax Explosion of 1917 was created using a mix of AI tools and manual editing. The process included researching the historical event, writing the script, generating AI narration, creating and refining images in Photoshop, turning them into video clips, and assembling everything with sound effects, music, and editing to produce a polished historical short.

About Me

A Multidisciplinary Creator

Working at the intersection of music, AI, and visual design.

I'm a multidisciplinary creative focused on experimentation, digital aesthetics, and storytelling through sound and visuals. My work spans music production, AI-generated art, video editing, brand identity, and creative technology. Every project is an opportunity to push the boundaries of what's possible when creativity meets technology.

I believe in crafting experiences that feel alive — where every pixel, every note, and every frame serves a purpose. My approach combines technical precision with artistic intuition, resulting in work that resonates on both an emotional and aesthetic level.
